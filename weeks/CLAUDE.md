# /weeks

"Life in weeks" — visualise your life as a grid of weeks. Enter a birth date to see weeks spent and weeks remaining.

## Structure

Single page (`/weeks/index.html`) with a multi-step flow:
1. Input step — birth date entry
2. Visualisation step — rendered week grid

Steps are toggled via `.step` / `.step.active` classes with `fadeUp` animation between them.
